Welcome to the CommuniCaid.

CommuniCaid is an interactive and dynamic way to enhance the accessibility of stores and companies to people suffering from hearing impairment and/or muteness.

This iPad application takes advantage of Watson, a powerful speech recognition system created by AT&T, to transform speech into text.
